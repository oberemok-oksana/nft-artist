Kiwa hirsuta in dirty water

With this work I want to draw attention to the pollution of rivers, seas and oceans. In the center of the composition is a very unusual crab - Kiwa hirsuta (lat.) (Yeti-Krabbe). It is amidst the mud and can barely move. And the worst thing is that it feels organic in it.
